Main Characters are:

Kelly Quinn:
Kelly is always willing to help out others, especially her friends and her grandmother. She especially cares for her grandmother, and does everything she can to save her in Season 1. She is very strong-willed and doesn't let anything get to her head.

Darbie O'Brien:
Darbie O'Brien is one of the three main characters in Just Add Magic and is a protagonist in the series and a tritagonist of Mystery City. She has two best friends: Kelly Quinn and Hannah Parker-Kent whom are former protectors along with her.

Hannah Parker-Kent:
Hannah is a smart and intelligent person. She's very mature and is always organized, in contrast to her friend, Darbie. She keeps a strict schedule although she never feels tired.
